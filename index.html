Projects

A 3-Page Report of Sales Data in Power BI

This report analyzes the business's Profit or Loss in the last two years by period. It has a map visual to filter by store locations. It also shows the store performance by product category and subcategory, with revenue, cost, and margin data. In addition, it presents a seller's report with the most popular items based on sales volume, growth rate, and customer satisfaction. Lastly, I highlighted product categories that need to be investigated and noted my recommendations.

Bellabeat Business Analysis with R

This analysis was carried out in order to enhance Bellabeat's marketing strategy To do this, I studied trends and patterns in how customers utilize non-Bellabeat smart devices in order to apply these findings to Bellabeat's marketing approach. This investigation provides insights into current market developments, client preferences, and Bellabeat's competitive advantages. I also made some suggestions for strengthening the marketing approach and increasing sales and profits.

Call Centre Trends: Visualising Agent and Customer Behaviour (Power BI)

A Call Center Manager is looking for transparency and relevant insights into their customer's datasets. I created a dashboard that displays essential Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and metrics from their customer dataset. This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the call center's performance, such as the number of calls handled, the average call duration, the customer satisfaction rate, the agent occupancy rate, and the service level. It also allows the manager to filter and drill down into the data by various dimensions, such as time, agent, department, and customer segment. Lastly, the dashboard helps the manager to monitor and improve the call center's efficiency and quality of service.

Customer Churn Analysis (Power BI)

A Retention Manager in a telecom business is concerned at the rate of client attrition. Currently, the retention department gest in touch after the customer has terminated the contract, but this is reactionary: it would be better to know in advance who is at risk. I created a dashboard for the retention manager that displays key performance indicators and client attrition patterns. This dashboard helps to identify why customers are leaving and also provides insights into client behavior and preferences. I also presented my findings and proposed some actions to improve retention rates and customer satisfaction.

Diversity And Inclusion (Power BI)

Human resources at a telecom business places a high value on diversity and inclusion. They've been working hard to increase gender balance in upper management, but they haven't seen any results. I produced a report to visualize the organization's diversity and inclusion across departments and levels. I also pointed out the reason for the poor progress in establishing gender balance at the executive level.

Suicide Rates Analysis(1985 to 2016)

I examined a global dataset of suicide records accumulated from 1985 to 2016. This assembled dataset was created by combining four additional datasets that were connected by time and location in order to identify signals associated with higher suicide rates among different cohorts internationally, across the socioeconomic spectrum.
